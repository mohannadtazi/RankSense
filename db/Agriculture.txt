Title: Agriculture  
Introduction:  
Agriculture involves cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising livestock to produce food, fiber, and other products for human use.  

Core Topics:  
1. Sustainable Farming Practices  
2. Crop Production and Management  
3. Livestock Breeding and Animal Husbandry  
4. Agricultural Technology: Drones, Irrigation Systems  
5. Global Food Security Challenges  

Applications:  
Agriculture supports rural economies, ensures food security, and provides raw materials for industries like textiles and biofuels.
