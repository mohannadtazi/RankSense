Title: Economics  
Introduction:  
Economics is the study of how societies allocate scarce resources to meet their needs and wants. It analyzes production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services.  

Core Topics:  
1. Microeconomics: Demand, Supply, Market Equilibrium  
2. Macroeconomics: Inflation, Unemployment, GDP  
3. International Trade and Globalization  
4. Behavioral Economics  
5. Fiscal and Monetary Policies  

Applications:  
Economics plays a vital role in decision-making for governments, businesses, and individuals, shaping policies and investments.
