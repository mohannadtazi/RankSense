Title: Cybersecurity
Introduction:
Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. It is critical in safeguarding sensitive information and ensuring business continuity.

Core Topics:
1. Types of Cyber Threats: Malware, Phishing, Ransomware
2. Network Security and Firewalls
3. Encryption and Cryptography
4. Risk Assessment and Management
5. Incident Response and Recovery

Applications:
Cybersecurity measures are essential in industries such as banking, e-commerce, healthcare, and government to prevent data breaches and financial losses.
